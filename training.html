Training Record Management System

A comprehensive solution for managing organizational training programs and participant records

Laravel PHP MySQL Git

Project Overview

The Training Record Management System (TRMS) is a robust web application designed to streamline the management of training programs and participant records. This platform serves as an essential tool for organizations looking to enhance their training processes, track employee development, and maintain comprehensive records of training activities.

Key Features

User-Friendly Interface

The TRMS features an intuitive and responsive design, making it easy for administrators, trainers, and participants to navigate the platform and access relevant information.

Training Program Management

Administrators can create, update, and manage training programs, including details such as program descriptions, schedules, locations, and prerequisites. This centralized management system simplifies the organization of training activities.

Participant Registration

Employees can easily register for training programs through the platform. The registration process is streamlined, allowing participants to view available programs and sign up with minimal effort.

Attendance Tracking

The system includes functionality for tracking participant attendance during training sessions. Administrators can monitor who attended each session, ensuring accurate record-keeping and accountability.

Performance Assessment

Trainers can evaluate participants through assessments, quizzes, or feedback forms. The system captures performance data, allowing organizations to measure the effectiveness of training programs and identify areas for improvement.

User Roles and Permissions

The platform supports multiple user roles, including administrators, trainers, and participants. Each role has specific permissions, ensuring that users can only access the information relevant to their responsibilities.

Mobile Optimization

The system is fully optimized for mobile devices, enabling users to access training information, register for programs, and complete assessments from anywhere at any time.
