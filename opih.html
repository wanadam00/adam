Project Overview

Opih is a comprehensive Human Resource Management System designed to streamline HR processes, manage employee data, and facilitate efficient recruitment. The platform aims to simplify HR operations, making it easier for organizations to handle employee management, talent acquisition, and administrative tasks. With a focus on user experience and functionality, Opih serves as a valuable tool for HR professionals and management teams.

Key Features

User-Friendly Interface

The system features an intuitive and modern design that enhances user navigation. HR professionals can easily manage employee data, while managers can navigate the platform to oversee recruitment and administrative tasks.

Advanced Recruitment Search

HR teams can search for candidates using various filters, including job title, location, industry, and experience level. This functionality allows recruiters to quickly find suitable candidates that match job requirements and organizational needs.

Employee Profile Management

HR professionals can manage comprehensive employee profiles including personal information, skills, work experience, and performance records. This centralized system enables efficient employee data management and quick access to candidate information during recruitment.

Recruitment Tracking

Opih includes a comprehensive recruitment tracking system that allows HR teams to monitor candidate applications, track interview progress, and manage the entire hiring pipeline efficiently, streamlining the recruitment process.

Employer Dashboard

Employers have access to a dedicated dashboard where they can post job listings, review applications, and communicate with candidates. This centralized system enhances the recruitment experience and improves efficiency.

Resume Builder

The platform features a user-friendly resume builder that guides job seekers through the process of creating professional resumes. This tool helps candidates present their qualifications effectively.

Notifications System

Users receive notifications about new job postings, application updates, and messages from employers, ensuring they stay informed throughout the job application process.

Mobile Optimization

Opih is fully optimized for mobile devices, allowing users to search and apply for jobs on-the-go, making the job search process more accessible.
