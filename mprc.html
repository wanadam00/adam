Project Overview

The Malaysia Petroleum Resources Corporation (MPRC) Procurement System is a critical enterprise application designed to streamline and manage procurement processes for the organization. I was responsible for maintaining and fixing bugs in this Laravel-based system deployed on AWS infrastructure. The system handles complex procurement workflows, vendor management, contract processing, and financial approvals, ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulatory requirements.

Key Features

User-Friendly Interface

The system features an intuitive and modern design that enhances user navigation for managing procurement processes efficiently.

Procurement Workflow

Comprehensive workflow management for procurement requests, approvals, and processing with multi-level authorization and tracking.

Vendor Management

Complete vendor database with registration, qualification, and performance tracking to ensure reliable supplier relationships.

Contract Management

Digital contract lifecycle management including creation, negotiation, approval, and renewal tracking.

Financial Integration

Integration with financial systems for budget tracking, invoice processing, and payment management.

Reporting & Analytics

Advanced reporting capabilities for procurement analytics, spending analysis, and performance metrics.
